Iron Man

Tony Stark, a billionaire genius inventor, created the Iron Man suit to save his life and become a high-tech superhero. Known for his wit, intelligence, and resourcefulness, Iron Man is a founding member of the Avengers.

Captain America

Steve Rogers, enhanced to the peak of human potential through the Super Soldier Serum, wields his iconic Vibranium shield to fight for justice and freedom. A natural leader and moral compass, he is the heart of the Avengers.

Thor

The Norse God of Thunder and son of Odin, Thor wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir. With his godly strength and power over lightning, Thor protects both Asgard and Earth from cosmic threats.

Black Widow

Natalia Romanova, aka Black Widow, is a master spy and skilled fighter. Her expertise in espionage and combat makes her a critical member of the Avengers, despite her lack of superpowers.

Hulk

Dr. Bruce Banner transforms into the Hulk when provoked. With near-limitless strength and durability, the Hulk is both a powerful ally and a destructive force.

Spider-Man

Peter Parker, a teenager bitten by a radioactive spider, gained spider-like abilities. Balancing heroism and personal struggles, Spider-Man embodies the mantra, "With great power comes great responsibility."

Black Panther

T'Challa, king of Wakanda, protects his nation as the Black Panther. With enhanced abilities and Vibranium technology, he is a fierce warrior and a wise leader.

Doctor Strange

Dr. Stephen Strange, a former neurosurgeon, became the Sorcerer Supreme to defend Earth from mystical threats. With his mastery of the mystic arts, Strange is a key protector of the multiverse.

Scarlet Witch

Wanda Maximoff, also known as Scarlet Witch, possesses immense magical abilities and reality-warping powers. Her journey of self-discovery and redemption defines her path as a complex hero.

Ant-Man

Scott Lang, the Ant-Man, uses a suit that allows him to shrink in size, grow large, and control ants. With his charm and ingenuity, he plays a crucial role in the Avengers.

Loki

The God of Mischief and Thor’s brother, Loki is both a cunning antagonist and a reluctant ally. His complex character often straddles the line between villainy and heroism.

Guardians of the Galaxy

This motley crew of misfits, led by Star-Lord, includes Gamora, Drax, Rocket, and Groot. Together, they protect the galaxy from interstellar threats.

Captain Marvel

Carol Danvers, a former Air Force pilot, gains cosmic powers after being exposed to Tesseract energy. She is one of the most powerful Avengers and a defender of Earth and the universe.

Falcon

Sam Wilson, the Falcon, uses advanced wing technology to fight alongside the Avengers. He carries on the legacy of Captain America.

Winter Soldier

Bucky Barnes, a former brainwashed assassin, seeks redemption as the Winter Soldier. His vibranium arm and combat skills make him a formidable ally.
